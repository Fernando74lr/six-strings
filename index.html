Six strings

what is this?

This is a personal project that I wanted to start a long time ago, but I had not encouraged myself and had time to do the covers. I strive for each video to be better than the previous one while choosing good songs.

what kind of content do i play?

The type of genre that I choose for this project are mostly: classic rock, indie, pop, among others. But if you ask for something different I'd try to play it for you :)

HOW OFTEN DO I UPLOAD CONTENT?

I try to upload new content every Tuesday but sometimes it takes me longer to record the audio/video and edit everything on my own. I'm new at this but I'm tryng my best to create quality content. Please compare the first video with the newest video to give you an idea of my progress.
